Abstract

Project Overview & Goals

MY GARAGE is a smart web-based platform developed to help users easily find garages, book services, and manage vehicle maintenance in Bahrain. The system improves communication between customers and garage owners by providing booking management, dashboards, notifications, complaints, and an AI Garage Assistant for simple vehicle guidance.

Objectives

Simplify garage booking
Improve customer-garage communication
Provide dashboards for users
Support payment and notifications
Manage complaints
Integrate AI Garage Assistant

Methodology & Approach

The project followed an incremental development approach. The team started with planning and requirement collection, followed by interface design, database design, implementation, testing, and final deployment.

Conclusion & Future Work

MY GARAGE provides a smart and organized solution for garage booking and management in Bahrain. Future improvements may include a mobile application, real online payments, GPS integration, customer reviews, and smarter AI vehicle diagnostics.
